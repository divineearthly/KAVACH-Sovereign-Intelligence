# KAVACH: Sovereign AI Cyber Defense
## Executive Summary — GUSEC NIDHI-SSS Application

### The Problem
Indian citizens lost over Rs.10,000 crores to cyber fraud in 2025. 
Phishing SMS, vishing calls, and UPI scams target rural and semi-urban 
populations who lack access to enterprise security tools. Global spam 
filters fail because they don't understand Hinglish, regional bank names, 
or state-specific threat patterns.

### Our Solution
KAVACH is a sovereign, on-device cyber defense system that maps 64 Vedic 
Sutras to modern AI primitives. It runs on entry-level Android phones 
(Rs.8,000) without any cloud dependency.

### Key Metrics
- 2.4M events/sec throughput on ARM64
- 73.7% phishing detection rate
- 0% false positives
- 124 detection patterns including Hinglish
- DPDPA 2023 compliant by design

### Market Opportunity
- 1.2 billion mobile users in India
- Rs.10,000+ crore annual fraud market
- Zero domestic competition in sovereign, on-device security
- Government push for Aatmanirbhar Bharat in cybersecurity
