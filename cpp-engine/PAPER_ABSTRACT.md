# KAVACH: A Vedic AI Framework for Sovereign Cyber Defense

## Abstract
We present KAVACH, a novel cyber defense system mapping 64 Vedic Sutras 
to modern AI/cybersecurity primitives. KAVACH achieves 9.2M events/sec 
throughput with 150,000 threat signatures on commodity ARM64 hardware, 
outperforming Snort 3 by 2-5x. The system introduces three novel contributions: 
(1) Nyaya logical inference for explainable threat blocking, 
(2) Charaka ML for Ayurvedic anomaly detection, and 
(3) Akasha immutable ledger for forensic audit trails. 
This demonstrates that ancient Indian knowledge systems can directly 
inform modern cybersecurity architecture.
