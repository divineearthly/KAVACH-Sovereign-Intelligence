
## 🔱 KAVACH: Sovereign Cyber Defense Intelligence

KAVACH (Sanskrit for "shield" or "armor") is an advanced, AI-driven cyber defense intelligence system inspired by the profound philosophical and scientific principles of Vedic Sutras. It provides a multi-layered, autonomous, and self-aware security framework designed to protect digital assets from sophisticated threats.

### 🛡️ KAVACH Modules:

1.	**CHARAKA — Behavioral Anomaly Engine:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Charaka Samhita (diagnosis and treatment of diseases).
	*	**Function:** Identifies deviations from established normal user/system behavior patterns, akin to detecting imbalances (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) in a healthy system. Employs Isolation Forest and One-Class SVM for robust anomaly detection.

2.	**NYAYA — Phishing Interceptor:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Nyaya Sutras (logic and inference).
	*	**Function:** Utilizes a 5-step inference chain (Pratijna, Hetu, Udaharana, Upanaya, Nigamana) and 'Rasas' (emotional manipulations) to logically detect and classify phishing and social engineering attempts in text.

3.	**GANDHARVA — Voice Deepfake Detector:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Gandharva Veda (music and harmonious sound).
	*	**Function:** Analyzes subtle spectral and harmonic characteristics of audio to discern real human voices from AI-generated or cloned synthetic voices, identifying the 'resonance signature' of authenticity.

4.	**AKASHA — Immutable Forensic Ledger:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Akashic Records (cosmic memory).
	*	**Function:** Creates a blockchain-style, append-only, tamper-evident ledger for all security events, ensuring immutable and verifiable integrity of forensic data.

5.	**SUSHRUTA — Autonomous Incident Response:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Sushruta Samhita (surgical procedures and precision).
	*	**Function:** Provides autonomous, surgical incident response actions based on threat levels, mapping them to Mandukya's four states of consciousness (JAGRAT, SVAPNA, SUSHUPTI, TURIYA) to dictate response severity.

6.	**DHARMA — Shadow AI Monitor:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Dharma (cosmic law, righteousness).
	*	**Function:** Acts as the ethical guardian, monitoring other AI models within KAVACH for data drift, model degradation, bias, and adversarial attacks, ensuring models uphold their intended purpose.

7.	**SARATHI — KAVACH Unified Dashboard:**
	*	**Vedic Inspiration:** Sarathi (charioteer/guide).
	*	**Function:** Offers a unified interface to monitor the health, threats, and responses across all KAVACH modules, aggregating information, visualizing metrics, and providing interactive exploration of the security posture.
